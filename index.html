Tested & Disliked

Energi Tea Ice

    Version 1

  • 10ml CHILL PILL - Truly Energy Drink
  • 10ml Big Mouth Classic - Ice Tea

  • 5ml Imperia BLACK LABEL - Menthol

  • This one was ruined due to the Menthol flavor and it's chemical taste.

  • Another issue was that the Ice Tea flavor from Big Mouth is quite bad tasting alone in my opinion.

    Version 2

  • 10ml CHILL PILL - Truly Energy Drink
  • 10ml CHILL PILL - Karma (Iced Lemon Tea)

  • 5ml Imperia BLACK LABEL - Koolada

  • Changed to the good ice flavor and different tea flavor.

  • Found out that these flavors cancel each other out almost fully and they make a weird peppermint flavor. So it tastes like a really subtle peppermint jelly (gelatine?).

Ice Flavors

Imperia BLACK LABEL - Koolada

  • A really nice Ice flavor. There's no real "flavor", just the feeling of freezing cold.

Imperia BLACK LABEL - Menthol

  • I cannot really recommend this to anyone. It's quite bad in my option.
  • As I was looking for a freezing feeling without any flavor, this is not it.
  • It made my mixes taste like chemicals (Air Refresheners, Laundry Detergent, etc.)
